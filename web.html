About VELANDEV

VELANDEV is a dynamic and visionary startup dedicated to identifying, nurturing, and empowering top talent in Full Stack Development and Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning. We are not just a development company—we are a problem-solving ecosystem built on innovation, collaboration, and impact.

At VELANDEV, we bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application. Our platform brings together aspiring technologists, developers, and data scientists to work on practical challenges that affect industries, communities, and the environment.

We believe in learning by doing. That’s why we focus on hands-on project development, real-time collaboration, and building solutions that solve meaningful problems. From designing scalable applications to deploying intelligent AI/ML models, our projects are deeply rooted in practical relevance and social responsibility.

What Do We Do?

Talent Recruitment & Mentoring: We scout and cultivate skilled individuals ready to grow through impactful, real-world experience.

Project-Based Learning: We engage our team in live, full-cycle projects across web development, AI/ML integration, and environmental tech solutions.

Real-World Impact: We apply technology to tackle tangible issues—especially in sustainability, automation, and community well-being.
